About Furniture Plus


Tradition since 2021

Furniture Plus was founded and created by Anthony Mitine for DV Hacks 3. Furniture Plus allows its users to be able to see our products in their houses without having to purchase it. Furniture Plus has a wide variety of products for your home, whether it's for a bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, or even your backyard. By using Echo AR, Furniture Plus enables its customers and users to place furniture in their homes.

Anthony at Furniture Plus understands that not everyone is able to go to stores to look at furniture for their homes because of the Covid-19 Pandemic, so he decided to bring the furniture to the people. Check out how to use our AR platform and the diversity of products! I hope you enjoy!

How to Use Our AR Platform


To use the Echo AR platform for Furniture Plus, you will have to use your smartphone as you will be needing a camera to scan all of the products. Each product name will be hyperlinked to a unique AR platform. Once you have accepted all of the terms for the service, follow the directions that are provided on your smartphone to be able to view the product in real time in your house!
